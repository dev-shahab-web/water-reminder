# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, collaborative, and professional environment for everyone who contributes to this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and receive constructive feedback professionally.
- Focus discussions on the project and its technical goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.
