# Water Reminder Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence

Water Reminder is a calm hydration companion. It should feel fresh, private, effortless, and quietly premium. The identity is water-inspired rather than generically blue: clarity, reflection, flow, ripples, and soft glass are the recurring visual ideas.

## Brand Attributes

- Calm: never urgent, loud, competitive, or guilt-based.
- Fresh: airy spacing, clean surfaces, clear contrast, hydrated color.
- Minimal: one clear focus at a time, with Home progress as the hero.
- Trustworthy: privacy-first, offline-first, no visual tricks.
- Premium restraint: polished details without decoration overload.
- Android-native: familiar Material 3 structure with distinct water-led identity.

## Brand Motto

Hydration should become a habit, not a task.

## Visual Personality

The product should look like cool clean water in a quiet glass, not a cartoon ocean. Use translucent teal, mist backgrounds, soft highlights, and measured motion. The interface should feel alive through small water behaviors: a surface ripple after logging, a gentle moving wave in the hero, a soft glow at completion.

## Core Visual Motifs

- Water drop: the primary recognition shape.
- Ripple line: the signature action mark.
- Circular progress: the daily habit loop.
- Glass surface: premium depth without heavy blur.
- Mist and night backgrounds: calm light/dark foundation.
- Small specular highlights: freshness and dimensionality.

## Logo Concept

The core mark is a rounded water drop with a calm ripple crossing the lower third. The drop should feel centered, stable, and modern. It should not look like a medical droplet, rain icon, map pin, flame, tear, or generic weather symbol.

Use:

- Single centered drop.
- Rounded drop apex.
- Broad, grounded lower bowl.
- One subtle wave/ripple cut or highlight.
- Aqua highlight on the lower left or center.
- Mist or deep teal container depending on use.

Avoid:

- Multiple droplets.
- Cartoon splash marks.
- Sharp water spikes.
- Overly realistic reflections.
- Complex gradients that fail at small size.

## Icon Concept

The app icon should be recognizable at 48dp. Use a soft mist background, centered teal drop, and a single horizontal ripple/highlight. Android adaptive icon foreground should keep the mark large enough to survive launcher masks.

Icon composition:

- Background: mist `#F7FBF8` or pale aqua `#DFF6F1`.
- Main drop: deep teal `#007A8A`.
- Highlight/ripple: aqua `#8DDDD3` and mist white `#F7FBF8`.
- Shape silhouette: simple enough for monochrome themed icons.

## Color System Summary

Water Reminder uses Material 3 with a custom teal/mist/night palette:

- Primary teal: `#007A8A`.
- Fresh aqua: `#8DDDD3`.
- Mist background: `#F7FBF8`.
- Deep night: `#101816`.
- Moss secondary: `#4E7B68`.
- Coral error: `#A73F2E` light, `#D95F45` dark.

The palette should never become one-note blue. Moss, mist, night, and subtle sand/clay accents keep the product grounded.

## Typography

Typography should feel modern, legible, and unobtrusive.

Preferred direction:

- Use the existing app font stack and Material 3 scale.
- Display/title text: confident, rounded, semi-bold to bold.
- Body text: readable, relaxed, with generous line height.
- Numeric hydration values: large, tabular-feeling, clean, high contrast.
- Microcopy: short sentences, no marketing flourish.

Do not use decorative scripts, condensed display faces, childish rounded fonts, or clinical monospaced systems for normal UI.

## Iconography Rules

Use Material Community Icons through the shared app icon system. Icons should be literal, familiar, and functional.

Rules:

- Use 20-22dp icons for most actions.
- Use 44-48dp touch targets for icon buttons.
- Use line icons for settings/actions.
- Use filled or brand-mark icons only for identity surfaces.
- Always include accessible labels for icon-only controls.

Avoid mixing icon families unless a formal brand update is approved.

## Motion Language

Motion should express water responding to touch:

- Gentle.
- Continuous only where meaningful.
- Low amplitude.
- Short response time.
- Never attention-seeking.

Signature action:

Tap quick add -> water rises -> surface ripples -> ring updates -> number transitions -> soft haptic -> concise success message.

Primary response should complete in about 300-500ms. Continuous water motion should pause when Home is not focused, app is backgrounded, or Reduce Motion is enabled.

## Glass Effect Rules

Glass effects should suggest clean water and depth, not frosted novelty.

Use:

- Low-opacity surface tint.
- Thin soft border.
- Subtle vertical gradient.
- Minimal shadow.
- Gentle inner highlight.

Avoid:

- Heavy blur.
- Floating decorative blobs.
- Neon cyan.
- Dense layered cards.
- Text readability loss.

## Widget Branding

The widget should feel like a miniature version of the app:

- Compact: brand mark only plus progress essentials.
- Medium/expanded: brand mark plus "Water Reminder".
- Preserve teal/aqua progress and calm surfaces.
- Keep quick-add actions clear and tappable.
- Use native widget-safe drawable resources.
- Stay readable in light and dark launcher surfaces.

## Splash Design

Splash should be quiet and premium:

- Mist background.
- Centered brand mark.
- No loading copy unless technically required.
- No animation dependency for initial native splash.
- Transition into app shell without visual jump.

## Play Store Visual Language

Store visuals should show real product utility, not abstract wellness art.

Show:

- Home progress hero.
- Quick add.
- Widget.
- Reminders.
- History/statistics.
- Optional Health Connect.
- Privacy/diagnostics toggle if useful.

Avoid:

- Medical claims.
- Before/after health promises.
- Stock water bottles as the main identity.
- Busy lifestyle photography.
- Fake charts or fake UI.

## Illustration Language

Illustrations, if used, should be sparse and product-adjacent:

- Soft water rings.
- Simple glass silhouettes.
- Gentle droplet geometry.
- Mist/aqua gradients.
- Rounded, geometric shapes.

Do not introduce characters, animals, mascots, loud gamification badges, beach/ocean scenes, or medical illustrations.

## Image Model Prompt Anchor

When asking an image model to create Water Reminder visuals, include:

> Calm premium Android hydration app identity, centered teal water-drop mark with one soft ripple, mist background, translucent aqua highlights, Material 3 minimalism, privacy-first wellness utility, no medical imagery, no cartoon splash, no neon blue, clean glass-like water surface.
