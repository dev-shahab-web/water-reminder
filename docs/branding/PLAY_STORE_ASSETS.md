# Play Store Visual Assets

## Visual Direction

Play Store assets should look like the product: calm, useful, private, and water-led. Use real UI and brand elements rather than generic wellness photography.

## 512 x 512 App Icon

Composition:

- Mist rounded-square background.
- Centered teal water drop.
- One soft ripple highlight across the lower third.
- Subtle aqua support circle or background field.
- Strong silhouette for small size.

Do not include text, shadows that muddy the silhouette, medical crosses, bottles, glasses, or extra droplets.

## 1024 x 500 Feature Graphic

Recommended concept:

- Mist-to-aqua calm background.
- Large brand drop/ripple mark on one side.
- Subtle water surface line or ripple rings.
- Optional cropped Home hydration ring UI on the other side.
- Very short text only if required: `Water Reminder`.

No medical claims, no "best app", no disease prevention, no busy lifestyle photography.

## Screenshot Set

Use 6-8 portrait screenshots:

1. Home hero with calm water progress.
2. Quick add and recent timeline.
3. Reminder controls.
4. Widget on Android launcher.
5. History.
6. Statistics.
7. Optional Health Connect explanation/connected state.
8. Settings privacy diagnostics toggle.

## Screenshot Composition

Rules:

- Real UI only.
- Use synthetic sample hydration data.
- Clean status bar.
- No personal notifications.
- No private Health Connect values.
- No raw IDs, errors, debug overlays, or development launcher.
- Capture light and dark themes in balance.
- Keep captions short and truthful.

Caption style:

- "Log water in seconds."
- "Gentle reminders, never pressure."
- "Progress on your home screen."
- "Optional Health Connect sync."
- "Private by default."

Avoid:

- "All data stays on device."
- "Clinically accurate."
- "Prevents dehydration."
- "Guaranteed reminders."
- "Best water tracker."

## Device Frames

Device frames are optional. If used:

- Use one consistent Android device frame.
- Keep the app UI large enough to inspect.
- Do not cover important controls with captions.

## Illustration Add-ons

Allowed supporting illustration elements:

- Soft ripple rings.
- Thin curved water surface.
- Mist gradient panels.
- Subtle drop silhouette.
- Glass-like highlight.

Not allowed:

- Characters/mascots.
- Cartoon splashes.
- Beach/ocean scenes.
- Medical diagrams.
- Fitness transformation imagery.
