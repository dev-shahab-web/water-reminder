Health & Fitness Android app

Calm hydration tracking, privacy-aware by design.

Water Reminder helps users log water, manage reminders, use Android Health Connect hydration sync, and review progress without requiring an account or login.

Read the Privacy Policy Get support

Local-first app data

Hydration logs, preferences, goals, reminder settings, and related app data are stored locally on the user's device.

Optional Health Connect

Water Reminder reads or writes hydration data through Android Health Connect only after explicit user permission.

Diagnostics default off

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics diagnostics are optional and controlled by an in-app setting that defaults to off.
