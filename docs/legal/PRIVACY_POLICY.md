# Privacy Policy

## Summary

Water Reminder is a local-first hydration reminder app. Core hydration tracking works without an account, backend, cloud sync, or internet connection.

If enabled in Settings, Water Reminder may share anonymous diagnostics and basic app usage events with Google Firebase to help improve stability and product quality.

Hydration content and Health Connect records are never included in telemetry.

## Data Stored On Device

Water Reminder stores the following locally on your device:

- Hydration entries.
- Daily goal.
- Reminder preferences.
- Theme and accessibility preferences.
- Optional Health Connect sync metadata.

## Health Connect

Health Connect is optional. If you connect it, Water Reminder requests hydration read/write access only.

Water Reminder may:

- Read hydration records from Health Connect.
- Write water you log in Water Reminder to Health Connect.
- Store Health Connect record metadata locally for duplicate prevention.

Water Reminder does not request unrelated health data.

## Anonymous Diagnostics

The Settings toggle `Share anonymous diagnostics` controls Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collection.

When enabled, Water Reminder may send:

- Basic screen names.
- Basic app action names.
- Crash diagnostics.
- Safe operational error categories.

Water Reminder does not send:

- Hydration amounts.
- Daily totals.
- Goals.
- Hydration history.
- Health Connect values.
- Health Connect record ids.
- Reminder schedules.
- Export/import contents.
- Email address.
- User-entered text.
- Raw SQL or database rows.
- Precise location.
- Account identifiers.

Firebase data retention and processing are controlled by Google Firebase settings.

## Accounts And Cloud

Water Reminder does not require an account and does not upload hydration logs to a Water Reminder backend.

## Permissions

Depending on features you use, Water Reminder may request:

- Notifications, for local reminders.
- Health Connect hydration read/write access, if you connect Health Connect.

## Contact

Use the feedback link in the app for privacy questions or support.
