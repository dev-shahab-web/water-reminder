Water Reminder is a general wellness utility for hydration habit tracking, reminders, history, statistics, widgets, and optional Health Connect hydration synchronization.

## User Estimates

Hydration amounts are user estimates. Cup, bottle, and custom quantities may be approximate. Statistics, streaks, and progress are based on the data available locally in the app and may not be complete.

## Reminders

Reminders are intended as gentle prompts. Android, device manufacturers, battery settings, Focus/Do Not Disturb modes, notification permissions, time changes, and operating-system behavior may delay, suppress, or remove notifications.

## Health Connect

Health Connect is optional and Android-only. Availability, permissions, data accuracy, and sync behavior depend on Android Health Connect, user permissions, and other apps that may read or write hydration records.

Water Reminder requests hydration read/write access only. Imported hydration may come from other apps. Water Reminder uses duplicate-prevention metadata, but users should still review imported data if accuracy matters to them.

## Local Data And Backups

Water Reminder is offline-first and stores core hydration data locally. Local data can be lost if the app is uninstalled, device storage is cleared, the device is lost, or backups are unavailable. Exported files are controlled by the user.

## Optional Telemetry

The `Share anonymous diagnostics` setting controls optional Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collection. When enabled, anonymous usage events and crash diagnostics may be sent to Google Firebase. Hydration records and Health Connect data are excluded from telemetry.
