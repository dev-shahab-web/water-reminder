## Full Description

Water Reminder helps you build a simple hydration habit without turning wellness into another noisy app.

Log water in seconds, see today's progress at a glance, use gentle local reminders, review history and statistics, and keep progress visible with an Android home-screen widget.

Water Reminder is offline-first and does not require an account. Hydration records and Health Connect data are stored locally and are never included in telemetry. If you choose to enable anonymous diagnostics, basic usage events and crash diagnostics may be sent to Google Firebase to improve reliability.

Features:

- Fast quick-add and custom water logging.
- Calm Home dashboard with progress, recent entries, and undo.
- Local reminders with pause and quiet-hour controls.
- Hydration history and simple statistics.
- Android home-screen widget with quick actions.
- Optional Android Health Connect hydration read/write sync.
- Local export, import, reset, and delete controls.
- Light and dark themes.
- Accessibility and Reduce Motion support.
- No mandatory account, ads, subscriptions, or cloud hydration backend.

Water Reminder is a general wellness utility. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or disease-prevention guidance. Hydration amounts are user estimates, and reminders may be delayed or suppressed by Android or device settings.
