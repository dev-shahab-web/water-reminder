Water Reminder Privacy Policy

Water Reminder is a local-first hydration reminder app. Core hydration tracking works without an account, backend, cloud sync, or internet connection.

Anonymous diagnostics

If enabled in Settings, Water Reminder may share anonymous diagnostics and basic app usage events with Google Firebase. Hydration content and Health Connect records are never included.

Local data

Hydration entries, daily goals, reminder preferences, and app settings are stored locally on your device.

Health Connect

Health Connect is optional. If connected, Water Reminder requests hydration read/write access only and uses local metadata to prevent duplicates.

Telemetry exclusions

Water Reminder does not send hydration amounts, daily totals, goals, history, Health Connect record ids, reminder schedules, export/import contents, user-entered text, raw SQL, precise location, or account identifiers to telemetry.

Contact

Use the feedback link in the app for privacy questions or support.
