Effective date: July 14, 2026

General Wellness Use

Water Reminder is a general wellness utility for hydration habit tracking, reminders, history, statistics, widgets, and optional Health Connect hydration synchronization.

Water Reminder is not medical advice. It does not diagnose, treat, monitor, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. It is not a medical device or clinical decision support product. Ask a qualified health professional for medical guidance.

User Estimates

Hydration amounts are user estimates. Cup, bottle, and custom quantities may be approximate. Statistics, streaks, and progress are based on the data available locally in the app.

Reminders

Android, device manufacturers, battery settings, Focus/Do Not Disturb modes, notification permissions, time changes, and operating-system behavior may delay, suppress, or remove notifications.

Health Connect

Health Connect is optional and Android-only. Availability, permissions, data accuracy, and sync behavior depend on Android Health Connect, user permissions, and other apps. Water Reminder requests hydration read/write access only.

Local Data And Backups

Water Reminder stores core hydration data locally. Local data can be lost if the app is uninstalled, device storage is cleared, the device is lost, or backups are unavailable. Exported files are controlled by the user.

Optional Telemetry

The Share anonymous diagnostics setting controls optional Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collection. Hydration records and Health Connect data are excluded from telemetry.
